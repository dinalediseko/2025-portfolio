About Me

You can call me Didi. My professional identity, D'SEIKOU, is a fusion of my surname, Diseko, and the Japanese word for success, 'seikou'. It reflects my core beliefs and who I am at my essence. I spent three years studying towards a diploma in Integrated Communication Design at the Tshwane University of Technology, gaining hands-on experience in a variety of creative disciplines. My time there equipped me with a versatile skill set, spanning Interaction Design, Information Design, Photography, Motion Design, and Marketing, giving me a comprehensive understanding of the creative industry. Driven by a passion for continuous growth, I recently expanded my expertise into the realm of software development, merging my design background with technical skills to create innovative solutions.
